About Us
In a world full of new technologies, technology can play a crucial role in improving lives. People with disabilities. The "Glasses for the Blind" project comes as an example of the use of technology To enhance independence and improve the quality of life of individuals with visual disabilities. This will address Research the design and development of innovative smart glasses based on modern technologies and advanced programming tools.

NABD design by leading human performance experts are used from athletes, doctors and more. High technology designed for daily performance regardless of your health. NABD collects biometric data that has the biggest effect on your health to understand your health standards and fitness. Your assignment is translated for your goal and then studies your body patterns to train you on custom recommendations to adapt your lifestyle and habits to understand your potential. What NABD is different simple design and no screen or any unnecessary things to build more wearable technology in the market to make it more comprehensive to help you understand what to do. NABD is not only for athletes but for the daily wear of those interested in adding a structure for their health and fitness.
